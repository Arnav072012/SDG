The 3 SDG goal

Ensure that everyone has access to enough safe and nutritious food, and that people can acquire the food they need.
This goal also aims to improve nutrition, achieve food security, and promote sustainable agriculture.

Ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all ages.
This goal focuses on targets that improve the health of a country's population, especially in regions with the highest disease
burden and neglected population groups.

Ensure that everyone has access to water and sanitation, and that these resources are managed sustainably.
